How it works

Paste. See what's wrong. Fix it.

Most of ContextCrunch runs instantly in your browser. No waiting, no server, no account.

01 — instant

Paste your conversation

Copy any conversation from Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini and paste it in. Or upload a PDF, Word doc, PowerPoint, spreadsheet, image, or code file. Token count appears immediately.

02 — instant

See what's eating your context

See exactly how full your AI's memory is, how much of your conversation is repeated, how information-dense it is, and how much slower your AI is getting as the conversation grows.

03 — on request

Get a compressed version

Click Compress. Our AI finds the repeated and redundant parts, rewrites your conversation to be shorter without losing any meaning, and gives you a version ready to paste back in.

04 — optional

Understand the math

Curious why this happens? Every score links to a plain-English explanation with an optional technical deep dive. Learn how AI memory actually works — no CS degree needed.
