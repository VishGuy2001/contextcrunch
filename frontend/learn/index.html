Entropy & information theory

How do we measure information vs noise? Shannon entropy (a score for how surprising your text is) tells us how much is genuinely new vs repeated. Low entropy = lots of filler. High entropy = dense, hard to compress.

Quantization (making models smaller)

Attention & response speed

Why do long conversations slow down? Before answering, AI re-reads every message — and that work grows quadratically (double the tokens = 4× the compute). This is why compressing 30% of tokens makes responses ~2× faster, not just 30% faster.
